# Major Use Cases of Metasploit

Metasploit is widely used in various cybersecurity tasks. Here are its primary fields of application:

## 1 Penetration Testing
Metasploit helps simulate real-world attacks by exploiting known vulnerabilities in systems to assess security posture.

## 2 Vulnerability Scanning & Management
Though not a full scanner, Metasploit integrates with tools like Nexpose or Nessus to exploit discovered vulnerabilities.

## 3 Exploit Development
Security researchers use Metasploit to test custom exploits and payloads against different systems.

## 4 Social Engineering
Modules like `browser_autopwn` and phishing templates assist in crafting realistic social engineering attacks.

## 5 Post-Exploitation
Metasploit offers various post-exploitation modules for privilege escalation, data collection, and lateral movement.
